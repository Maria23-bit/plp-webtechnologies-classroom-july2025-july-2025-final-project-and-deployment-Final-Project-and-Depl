Our Verification Process

We take verification seriously to maintain trust with our community of travelers.

1. Initial Application

Submit detailed information about your business practices through our online form.

2. Documentation Review

Our team examines your sustainability claims and supporting documentation.

3. On-site Assessment

For qualifying businesses, we conduct an on-site evaluation (virtual or in-person).

4. Continuous Improvement

Verified businesses commit to ongoing sustainability improvements and reporting.

What We Look For

Environmental Sustainability Criteria

  • Energy efficiency measures and renewable energy use
  • Water conservation practices
  • Waste reduction and recycling programs
  • Sustainable sourcing and procurement
  • Carbon footprint measurement and reduction plans
  • Biodiversity protection efforts

Social Responsibility Criteria

  • Fair wages and working conditions for all employees
  • Local hiring and workforce development
  • Community engagement and support
  • Cultural preservation and respect
  • Accessibility and inclusivity
  • Guest education about local culture and environment

Economic Sustainability Criteria

  • Support for local suppliers and producers
  • Fair pricing practices
  • Long-term business viability
  • Contribution to local economy
  • Transparent business practices
  • Investment in community development

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I list my business on Ethosplore?

To list your business on Ethosplore, please visit our "For Businesses" page and fill out the application form. Our team will review your submission and contact you within 5-7 business days to discuss the verification process.

What are the sustainability criteria for businesses?

We evaluate businesses based on three main pillars: environmental practices, social responsibility, and economic sustainability. This includes factors like waste reduction, energy efficiency, fair labor practices, community engagement, and support for local economies.

How can I support Ethosplore as a traveler?

You can support us by using our platform to discover and book sustainable travel experiences, sharing your experiences with others, and providing feedback to businesses. Consider becoming a Ethosplore member to access exclusive content and features.

Do you offer partnerships for organizations?

Yes, we partner with sustainable tourism organizations, NGOs, and educational institutions. Please contact our partnerships team at partnerships@ethosplore.com to discuss collaboration opportunities.
